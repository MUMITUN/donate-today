Our Donate Plan

Plans have been laid to reach 40,000 families with financial support. The program addresses
food security, safe drinking water, sanitation, educational support.

Food for a family

Many kids in food insecure families receive free breakfast and lunch in school.we still plan to distribute holiday food packages to our families!

Shelter for a family

The project supports the housing restoration and re-building shelters among the families whose houses were fully submerged and or sunk by the water flooding.

Education for a child

we managed to help them get the textbooks and school supplies last September and to organise a school meal for all children who go to school for the whole school year.
